Jagrik

The "Be a Jagrik" project, primarily run by ComMutiny - The Youth Collective, is a citizen engagement initiative for adolescents and youth between 14 and 30 years old. It uses a game-based approach to make the Indian Constitution and Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) more accessible and relevant to their lives. Jagrik, which translates to "aware citizen," is a concept that combines "Jagruk" (aware) and "Nagrik" (citizen)

PMAS

The Online Personnel Management and Appraisal System for UNEP is a centralized digital platform designed to manage and streamline human resource operations across the organization. The system facilitates efficient handling of personnel data, including employee profiles, assignments, contracts, leave management, and performance records. By digitizing HR processes, the platform enhances data accuracy, improves accessibility for HR staff and management, and supports informed decision-making through comprehensive reporting and analytics features.

FOHRM

FOHRM (Field Office Human Resource Management) is an integrated online system developed to manage human resource operations across decentralized field offices. The platform supports the full employee lifecycle, including recruitment, onboarding, contract management, attendance, leave tracking, and performance evaluation. FOHRM ensures standardized HR practices, improves operational efficiency, and enhances data consistency across field locations, enabling centralized oversight and real-time access to critical HR information.
